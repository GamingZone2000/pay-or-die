Gameplay

Features

⛏️

Mine by Hand

Grab your pickaxe and smash rocks yourself. Every hit earns you coin — slow and steady if you're doing it alone.

👷

Hire Workers

Recruit workers who mine automatically. They take a small cut of the profits — but they work around the clock.

🏕️

Set Up Camp

Place your tent anywhere on the map. Sleep to skip the night — but only when you're safe near home.

🌄

Procedural World

Mountains, lakes, rivers and forests — every map is different. Find the best mining spots before the rent hits.

📈

Upgrades

Invest in better tools to mine faster and hit harder. Fall behind and you'll never catch up with the landlord.

💀

Game Over

Miss a payment and it's over. No second chances — the landlord has seen it all before.
